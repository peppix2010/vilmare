Who am I?

I’m Vilma. I'm almost there...half a century!
When I was 3 years old I used to scribble on papers saying I would become a math teacher one day,
when I was 13 I changed my mind thinking I would be an architect.
At 25 I became a biologist. At 30 I started building websites.
At 32 I was a Mom. At 40 I loved making ceramics, videos and drawings
… besides being a Mom, a Mom for two this time.

Today I feel that everything concerning the expression of the self,
of nature,
of taking care of others
is something strictly connected with who I am.

I am open to the new.
New things, new places, new people.
Life is a path that leads ahead.
I look behind when I need to anchor myself,
because roots are in the past.
And roots are important as much as dreams.
Dreams that give us the wings to fly forward.
